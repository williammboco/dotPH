What are Golden Visas anyway?

Golden Visas, also known as ‘Investors Residency’, are residency visas some countries (including Spain), issue to those who invest in the country. The Golden Visa not only grants the investor and their family residency and work permits, it also gives them access to the entire EU Zone.








No Requirements needed

With Spanish Golden Visas, there are no requirements to live in the country for a given period of time. Residents usually qualify for citizenship after 10 years of living in the country, but applicants from countries that were former Spanish colonies can qualify after just 5 years or even less. Filipino citizenship applicants can qualify after just two years.
